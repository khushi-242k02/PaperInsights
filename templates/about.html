About PaperInsights

PaperInsights is a cutting-edge web application designed to simplify and streamline the research process by providing quick summaries and entity recognition from academic papers. Developed using Flask and integrated with PyMuPDF and PyPDF2 for efficient PDF text extraction, it leverages the T5 transformer model for generating concise summaries from large text sections. The application also features a BERT-based NER pipeline that identifies and highlights key entities, such as names and locations, enhancing comprehension and data extraction. To further aid users, the tool includes word cloud visualizations that provide a clear overview of word frequency and content themes, making it easier to grasp the main ideas at a glance. With GPU acceleration for optimized performance, users can process extensive documents quickly. This makes the application a valuable asset for researchers, students, and professionals seeking efficient document review solutions.
